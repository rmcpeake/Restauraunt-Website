Pizzas

Pepperoni Pizza


Good old original pepperoni pizza, pizza dough with tomato sauce, mozzerella cheese, and pepperonis. You can't beat the basics with this delicious traditional pizza.

Supreme Pizza


This is just like the pepperoni pizza but with everything you could ask for on it. Pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozerella cheese, pepperoni, green peppers, mushroom, ham, and olives. If you are looking to make your night supreme this pizza is for you.

Bread Sticks

Regular Bread Sticks


Plain old traditional breadsticks. These tasty baked breadsticks are soft and delicious. They go great as a side with our pizza or calzones and will leave you satisfied.

Filled Bread Sticks


These filled bread sticks take a regular breadstick and enhance it to the next level. These are filled with gooey cheese and go perfectly with our pizza or some marinara sauce.

Calzones

Supreme Calzone


Fresh Homemade dough stuffed with a blend of provolone & mozzarella cheeses, pizza sauce, pepperonis, green peppers, onions, mushrooms, ham, and topped with grated parmesan cheese.
